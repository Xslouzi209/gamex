Delete Account

Last updated: February 15, 2026.

If you decide to stop using Gamex, you can permanently delete your account and the content linked to it.

1. How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Gamex.
  2. Go to your profile.
  3. Open the settings area.
  4. Tap "Delete account".
  5. Confirm the action to complete the process.

2. What happens after confirmation

  • Your account is permanently deleted.
  • Your personal library is removed.
  • Your reviews, ratings, and stats are erased.
  • This action cannot be undone.

3. If you cannot access the app

If you have lost access to your account or cannot open the app, you can request manual deletion by writing to support@slouzilabs.com.
